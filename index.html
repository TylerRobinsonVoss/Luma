AI-Powered Flashcards for Smarter Studying

Luma is an iOS app designed to make studying easier and more efficient. Create flashcards in seconds using AI by taking photos of your study notes, or manually input your content. Whether you're preparing for exams or learning something new, Luma helps you study smarter.

Features

AI Flashcard Creation

Take a photo of your study notes, and Luma’s AI will automatically generate flashcards for you.

Multiple Choice or Regular

Create standard flashcards or test your knowledge with multiple-choice questions.

Timed Study Sessions

Study with timed sessions to stay focused, or go at your own pace with regular mode.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your learning progress with detailed stats to see how far you’ve come.
